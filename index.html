03 — Who

Hi, I’m Robert.

Twenty years ago, I started as a designer. Since then I’ve been a strategist, researcher, founder, department head, and lone tinkerer — but the thread connecting all of it has stayed the same: solving hard problems with curiosity and creativity.

I spent a decade working with amazing teams at JKR, Unilever, and Pearlfisher on some of the world’s biggest, most distinctive brands — Kraft Heinz, Heineken, McDonald’s, Hilton. This year I relocated from New York to San Francisco to work directly with founders building something new.

The Innovation Department is where the work happens.

04 — The Process

Dig › Define › Design › Deliver

We start by listening — to you, your audience, and your category. Then we dig: into culture, into semiotics, into what’s missing in the market. From there we build together, iteratively, with your team involved every step of the way. The result is never just a strategy deck — it’s a shared belief in what your brand can be, and the tools to bring it to life.

What’s included at each stage

01

Dig

Listen & uncover
  • Cross-functional stakeholder & expert interviews
  • Human-centered, ethnographic consumer research
  • Semiotic analysis, cultural immersion, lateral category analysis
02

  • Opportunity identification & strategic territories
  • Strategic exploration and co-creation
  • Early concept testing and iteration
03

  • Creative exploration and development
  • Design finalization
04

Deliver

Launch & enable

  • Team education and engagement

Depending on the project, Robert is joined by a trusted team of designers, writers, and researchers — keeping the team lean and the thinking sharp.
